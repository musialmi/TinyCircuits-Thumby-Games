Let's play Bowling!!

Pitching Steps
1) Determine your stance
  Use the L/R buttons to move the girl left or right.
  (The starting position is not always the same.)
2) Aim
  Use the L/R buttons to move the arrow.
  The arrow is constantly wavering left and right.
  Time your press and press the A button to confirm.
  You can return to step 1 by pressing the B button.
  (You cannot go back once you’ve passed step 2.)
3) Determine Power
  The power meter is moving left and right.
  Time your press and press the A button to confirm.
4) Set the spin.
  The spin meter is moving from side to side.
  Time your press and press the A button to confirm.

This will start the throw.
There are no gutters in this game.
In cases like a split, you can attack with a curve from the very outside.
-------------------------------------- -
If you press the U button in Step 1 to view your scorecard.
Press the B button to return from the scorecard screen.

You can also press the B button while holding the D button in Step 1, the game will switch to a mode where you can view the ball and pins colliding from a bird’s-eye view.
(A circular icon will appear in the top-left corner when the mode changes.)
Since the positions of the ball and pins are easy to see, this also serves as a helpful hint for your strategy.
---------------------------------------
The game auto-saves after every throw, so even if you turn off the power, you can pick up where you left off next time.
